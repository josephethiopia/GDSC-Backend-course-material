Protection Against Common Attacks: helmet helps protect against various common web vulnerabilities like:
    Cross-Site Scripting (XSS): Prevents malicious JavaScript code from being injected into your web pages.
    Clickjacking: Stops attackers from embedding your website in a frame on another site to trick users into performing actions.
    Content Sniffing: Prevents browsers from automatically guessing the content type of files, potentially leading to vulnerabilities.
    Frame Options: Controls whether your website can be embedded in iframes, minimizing the risk of clickjacking.
